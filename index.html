Carrom

The game that brings family and friends together around a wooden board.

About Carrom

Carrom is a tabletop game that originated in South Asia. It involves flicking wooden discs into corner pockets using a striker. Over the years, carrom has become a popular indoor game played in homes, clubs, and competitions worldwide.

Quick Facts

  • The board is usually 74cm square.
  • There are 9 white, 9 black, and 1 red queen coin.
  • Professional tournaments are held globally.
